How To Play Twenty1

The aim of the game is to get as close to the number 21 as possible. If you go over 21 then you are bust (no longer in the round). The computer must gain a score of 16 or higher to stick. Player can stick on any number they choose. If the computer goes bust as well as the player, then no one wins the round. Numbers can only be used once per round (so if you receive an 8, then neither you or the computer can receive an 8 for the rest of the round. So try to remember what has come out previously to predict what numbers will be coming out!). Closest to 21 wins the round. First to win 5 rounds wins the game!.
